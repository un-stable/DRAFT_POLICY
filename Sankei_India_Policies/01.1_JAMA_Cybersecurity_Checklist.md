This subdocument is the **working self-assessment record** required by audit checklist item ⑥-1 ("Efforts towards Information Security"). It demonstrates that Sankei India proactively uses the JAMA Cybersecurity Checklist to assess, improve, and evidence its cybersecurity posture.

## 2. HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

1. For each checklist item below, review the requirement and score Sankei India's current state (1–5).
