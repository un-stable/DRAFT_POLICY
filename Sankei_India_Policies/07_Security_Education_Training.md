# SECURITY EDUCATION & TRAINING

**Organization:** Sankei India (sankei-india.com)  
**Document ID:** SIP-TRN-007  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026  
**Review Date:** March 1, 2027  

---

## 1. PURPOSE

Ensure all employees and relevant contractors receive appropriate information security awareness and role-based training to reduce human risk and improve incident response readiness.

---

## 2. SCOPE

All employees, contractors, and third parties with access to Sankei India systems.

---

## 3. TRAINING TYPES & FREQUENCY

- Mandatory Security Awareness e-learning: Annual (all employees) — include AD password hygiene and phishing reporting steps
- Role-based training: Onboarding and annual refresh for IT staff (AD administration, Sophos console operations, backup administration)
- Incident Response Tabletop Exercises: Semi-Annual (CSIRT) — include scenarios: Sophos-detected malware, AD compromise, public web server defacement
- Phishing simulations: Quarterly — target user base of ~90 endpoints
- Vendor security briefing: Annual

---

## 4. TRAINING TOPICS

- Password hygiene and MFA
- Phishing awareness and reporting via Sophos/phishing report channels
- AD account/security best practices (service account handling, privileged account management)
- Secure remote access and VPN usage
- Incident reporting procedures
- Basic secure maintenance for endpoints (patching, Sophos alerts)

---

## 5. RECORDS & METRICS

- Maintain training completion records in HR/LMS.
- KPIs: % completion rates, phishing click rates, time-to-report incidents.

---

## 6. RESPONSIBILITIES

- HR: Track training completion and enforce mandatory training.
- IT/Security: Develop and deliver technical training and exercises.
- Managers: Ensure team members complete required training.

---

## 7. EVALUATION

- Post-training quizzes to validate learning.
- Update training content annually or after major incidents.
